# What May and May Not Be Published on This Website

**Village at Ledge Stone Condominium Owners Association, Inc.** · governs everything served from `https://www.vlscoa.org`

This is a **public** website. Anything posted here is readable by anyone on the internet, forever, and is archived by third parties beyond the Association's control. The CMS publishes a save **directly to the live site** with no review gate — so this document, and the discipline of the officer using it, is the control.

**When in doubt, do not post it.** The public subset is intentionally narrow. Everything else is provided to owners **on request**, through the Secretary, under Tex. Prop. Code **§82.1141**.

## ✅ Allowed

- Recorded **dedicatory instruments**: the Condominium Declaration and its recorded amendments; the Community Manual (Certificate of Formation, Bylaws, Rules & Regulations, Assessment Collection Policy); the Design Guidelines; the Master Covenant and its amendments; the recorded plats.
- The recorded **management certificate**.
- The **adopted annual budget**.
- **Approved** meeting minutes — one per meeting. *(Approved only. Drafts never.)*
- Meeting **agendas** and meeting **notices**.
- **Board-adopted policies** (records production, payment plans, enforcement, etc.), once adopted.
- Community **announcements** of general application.
- **Role-based** contact addresses only: `board@`, `president@`, `secretary@`, `treasurer@`, `info@`, `support@` `@vlscoa.org`.

## 🚫 Never publish here

Confidential or withholdable under **§82.1141(j)–(k)** and Association policy:

- **Owner personal information** — names, personal email addresses, home addresses, phone numbers.
- The **member roster** / Owner Contact Registry, in whole or in part.
- **Delinquency, collections, or assessment-account** information for any owner.
- **Violation or enforcement** history for any owner.
- **Legal or privileged** material; anything subject to attorney-client privilege or litigation.
- **Executive-session** materials and deliberations.
- **Working or detailed financials**, individual owner ledgers, bank or payment details.
- **Vehicle, gate-code, or emergency-contact** data.
- **Personal** contact information for any director or officer *(use the role address)*.
- **Draft** minutes.

## Two rules that are easy to get wrong

**Posting is not statutory notice.** Official notices required under Tex. Prop. Code chs. 82 and 209 must still be **delivered to owners of record** by the required method. Posting to the website is a convenience and **does not satisfy or replace** the delivery requirement.

**The recorded instrument controls.** Every governing document on this site is a *convenience copy*. The authoritative version is the instrument recorded in the Official Public Records of **Hays County, Texas**. Reading copies must keep their "unofficial — the recorded instrument controls" banner and their link to the county record.
